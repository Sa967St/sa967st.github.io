Winter Variation

Printable Version

Winter Variation is used to insert the CE pair while orienting the LL corners, when the LL edges are oriented. The algorithms are from here.

  • R U' R'
  • (R U R' U') R U' R'
  • (R U R' U')*2 R U' R'
  • (R U R'2) (U' R2)*2 U2 R
  • (R U' R2) (U' R U' R') U2 R
  • (R U' R2) (U2 R U R') U R
  • (R U' R' U2) (R U2 R' U') R U' R'
  • U R U2 R'
  • (U R U' R') U R U2 R'
  • (U R U' R')*2 U R U2 R'
  • (U' R U' R') (U2 R U' R') U2 R U R'
  • (U R U2 R2) (U2 R U R') U R
  • (U R' U' R2) U' R2 U2 R
  • (U R U2 R2) (U' R U' R') U2 R
  • R U2 (L U L') R' U2 (L U L')
  • L' U2 (R U R') U2 L
  • L' U2 (R U' R') U2 L (U R U' R')
  • L' U2 L (R U R' U) L' U L
  • L' U2 R U (L U' L' U) R' U2 L
  • U R' U (L U' R2 U L') U R'
  • U' L' U R U' M' x'
  • U (L' U R U' L) U2 R'
  • U L' U (R U' R') L U' (R U' R')
  • U M x (U R' U' L) R U2 R'
  • U2 L U' (R U L' U R')
  • R2 D (R' U' R) D' R2
  • R2 D (R' U R) D' R' U2 R'
